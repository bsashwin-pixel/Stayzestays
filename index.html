What is Stayze

Not a hotel. Not an app. The operating system behind a great homestay.

Chikmagalur has beautiful properties and generous hosts. What it often lacks is the structure that turns a nice house into a consistent guest experience.

Stayze partners with property owners to bring that structure — listings, pricing, guest communication, hospitality standards — while owners keep full ownership of their homes.

  • We are not a hotel chain
  • We are not an OTA or booking aggregator
  • We do not buy or rent properties
  • We are the system that makes a good home run like a brand

For Property Owners

Your home earns more. You do less.

Most homestay owners in Chikmagalur are leaving money on the table — not because their property isn't good enough, but because managing the online side is a full-time job. Stayze handles that job for you.

Listing and photography

We photograph your property and create a listing that actually converts. Written to make the right guests book, and priced to perform.

Guest communication

Every inquiry, booking confirmation, and check-in message is handled by Stayze. You never have to stay glued to your phone.
